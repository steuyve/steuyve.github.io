The Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality

Note: this is mostly just to see if I got MathJax working correctly on this site.

Let $u$ and $v$ be vectors in $\mathbb{R}^n$. Then the Cauchy-Schwarz inequality states: $$\begin{equation}\left(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}u_i v_i\right)^2 \leq \left(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}u_i^2\right)\left(\sum\limits_{i=1}^{n}v_i^2\right).\end{equation}$$
