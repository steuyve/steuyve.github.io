When \(v\) is a positive solution to

\[ \frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t} = (p-1)^{p-1}\text{div}(|\nabla v|^{p-2}\nabla v), \](0.1)

and \(\varphi\) is defined such that

\[ v^{p-1} = C \frac{e^{-\varphi}}{t^{n/p}}, \]

Where \(C\) is some constant depending on the dimension \(n\) and \(p\). When the entropy is defined as

\[ \mathcal{W}_p(\varphi,t) = \int_M (t|\nabla\varphi|^p + \varphi - n)v^{p-1}d\mu, \]

after some calculation, we have that

\[\begin{aligned} \frac{d}{dt}\mathcal{W}_p(\varphi,t) &= \int_M \left[|\nabla\varphi|^p v^{p-1} - tp|\nabla\varphi|^p\frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t} - tp \left\langle \nabla \frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t}, \nabla\varphi\right\rangle\right] d\mu \\ &\qquad + \int_M \left[ -\frac{n}{pt}v^{p-1} - (n+1 - \varphi)\frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t}\right]d\mu. \end{aligned}\](0.2)

where we have worked out that

\[ \frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t} = -v^{p-1}|\nabla \varphi|^{p-2}\Delta\varphi + v^{p-1}|\nabla\varphi|^p - v^{p-1}\langle\nabla|\nabla\varphi|^{p-2},\nabla\varphi\rangle, \]

and

\[ \frac{\partial\varphi}{\partial t} = -\frac{1}{v^{p-1}}\frac{\partial v^{p-1}}{\partial t} - \frac{n}{pt}. \]
